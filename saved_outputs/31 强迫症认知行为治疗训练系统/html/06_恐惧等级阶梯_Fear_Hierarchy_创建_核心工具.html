How to Create Your Fear Hierarchy

  1. List situations, thoughts, or objects that trigger your OCD symptoms
  2. Rate each item's distress level (SUDS) from 0-100
  3. Describe any compulsions or avoidance behaviors
  4. The system will automatically sort items by distress level
  5. Use this hierarchy to guide your exposure exercises
